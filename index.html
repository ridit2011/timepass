ABOUT THE ARAVALLIS

Stretching across Rajasthan, Haryana and Gujarat, the Aravalli Range is one of the oldest mountain systems on Earth. In Gurugram it acts as a protected ecological corridor that preserves green views, cleaner air and a quieter living environment.
